About Platform

The Golf Charity Platform connects players with charities through fun and competitive golf events. Every game contributes to a greater cause ❤️. Players can join or host events, compete with others, and track their performance while supporting meaningful initiatives. The platform ensures transparency in donations and makes it easy to contribute towards social impact. Whether you're a golf enthusiast or someone who wants to make a difference, this platform brings together sport and purpose in one place. ❤️
